# What's changed

Plain-language notes on what's new in Enough, newest first. (Built rapidly in one
session, so the dates cluster — the version numbers show the order things shipped.)

## v0.11 — See what moving actually saves you

- **The stay-or-go comparison.** Tell us where you live now ("Where are you now?") and
  every city shows what it would *free up* and how many years *sooner* you could stop
  working — e.g. *"vs San Francisco: $6.2M less, 23 years sooner."* The cheapest path
  isn't an abstract number anymore; it's a decision.
- **Punchier share previews** — the preview card now has a clear call-to-action and a
  fuller title.

## v0.10 — Shareable, with a real preview card

- **Rich share previews.** Share your scenario and the link now unfurls into a clean
  card — your city, your number, and how close you are — so it invites a click instead
  of looking like a random URL.
- **Anonymous, cookieless analytics** (behind the scenes) so we can tell whether sharing
  actually works and what to improve next. No personal data, no tracking cookies.

## v0.9 — Faster to use, and filterable

- **Quick and Advanced modes.** You now start with just the essentials. Tap **Advanced**
  when you want to fine-tune build quality, expected returns, withdrawal rate, your age,
  and move-in costs.
- **Filter by country.** Show only India, only the US, or both — and the big headline
  updates to the cheapest option in whatever you're viewing.
- **Type exact numbers.** Salary, net worth, and move-in costs now take a typed value,
  not just the slider — handy for precise figures.
- **New city: San Diego** added to the US list.

## v0.8 — Numbers you can trust

- **Separate India and US salaries.** Your pay usually differs by country, so we ask for
  each on its own. The result is a far more realistic "enough" number — earning ₹ in
  India covers Indian costs very differently than a converted US salary did.
- **"Show the math" on every city.** Tap it to see exactly how a number is built:
  yearly spending, the invested nest egg, and upfront costs — all the way to the total.
- **Honesty next to the answer.** A short caveat now sits with the result: these are
  asking-side estimates (real deals often close lower) and simplified taxes — not advice.

## v0.7 — Easier to understand

- **A header that explains itself.** The page now says, in plain words, what it does and
  who it's for.
- **A plain-English summary.** A few sentences recap your whole situation and the result,
  so you don't have to read every control to get the picture.
- **Tap-to-learn everywhere.** An ⓘ on each control explains what it means, and the
  funding bars are clearly labeled as read-outs (not sliders).

## v0.6 — First version

- The core idea: set your situation, and see India and US cities ranked by the total
  you'd need to stop working — with how close you are today. Shareable by link, works on
  your phone, light or dark.
